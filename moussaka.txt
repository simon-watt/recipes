Ingredients
- lamb mince
- tin of tomatoes
- onion
- garlic
- oregano
- olive oil
- bay leaves
- milk
- eggplants
- parmesan cheese

Method
1. Make bolognese-like sauace
2. Make bechemal sauce with infused milk
3. Cook sliced eggplant
4. Season
5. Make five layers - meat, eggplant, meat, eggplant, bechemal sauce
6. Cook until done
